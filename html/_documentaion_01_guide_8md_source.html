35 ### Job Templates {#sec-job-templates}
36 
37 There are a number of example jobs in the RushTemplates directory.
38 
39 You can use them from the RushTemplates directory or copy them into your own jobs directory and customize them.
40 
41 To see a list of the jobs in the job templates folder run:
42 
43  drush rush-list --show=jt
44 
45 To see a see a description of the job with information on how it works run:
46 
47  drush rush <job-name> --show=help
48 
49 For example, to see how tests.filesystem works run:
50 
51  drush rush tests.filesystem --show=help
